Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a beautiful city known for its stunning temples, traditional tea houses, and breathtaking natural beauty. It’s the cultural heart of Japan with serene temples, gardens, and historic lanes that transport you back in time.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Fushimi Inari Shrine – famous for its thousands of vermillion torii gates.
  2. Kinkaku‑ji (Golden Pavilion) – a shimmering zen temple reflected in a tranquil pond.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove – walk through an otherworldly sea of bamboo.
  4. Gion District – the historic geisha quarter with traditional machiya houses.
  5. Philosopher’s Path – a peaceful cherry blossom-lined canal-side stroll.

Must‑Try Foods

  • Matcha (green tea) paired with wagashi (traditional sweets).
  • Kaiseki Ryori – an elegant multi-course seasonal dining experience.
  • Yudofu – a simple tofu hot pot popular near temple districts.
  • Nishin Soba – soba noodles topped with sweet simmered herring.
  • Yatsuhashi – Kyoto’s iconic cinnamon-flavored confection.
